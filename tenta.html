The Tentabrella

The Tentabrella is a main weapon that hits hard and slow and is a heavyweight, meaning that you move slower than both middleweight and lightweight weapons. The Tentabrella is the most powerful and slowest out of all the brellas. Its slow fire rate balances out the high damage output with the ability to almost or entirely one-shot bosses like Scrappers or Steel Eels. The canopy of the brella is the widest out of the three brellas and can be used in hallways like on Gone Fission Hydroplant shown here. It is not recommended to do egg delivery or crowd control because the weight of the weapon means you can't deliver eggs efficiently and the slow fire rate will gaurantee a quick death from being swarmed. Bosses that aren't efficient at being splatted by the Tentabrella are Stingers, Flyfish, Flipper Floppers, and Fish Sticks because the slow fireate and its inefficient ink cosumption will make it harder to splat those bosses than others. Fighting lessers isn't a good idea but using your canopy to push them back to keep the work site clear can help your teammates live longer and collect more eggs.
