The 96. Gal

The 96. Gal is a powerhouse when it comes to accuracy and range. This weapon is a middleweight meaning that you move faster than a heavyweight but slower than a lightweight. The 96. Gal hits like a tank and also moves like one. Unfortunately, Its high damage, accuracy, and range make you move extremely slow while firing so be sure that you're in a relatively safe spot where you can get out if there is any trouble that comes your way. Its slow fire rate but high damage make it great at dealing with technical bosses like Steelheads, Steel Eels, Slammin' Lids, and Drizzler balloons. It should not be used to take out lessers except Cohocks as its poor fire rate and slow movement speed while firing will not be helpful for you or your teammates so be sure to target bosses and let your teammates deal with the lessers for you. Do try to avoid trying to take down Stingers by yourself as being at the shoreline is not only dangerous by itself, but it's even more dangerous when your weapon doesn't handle swarms very well, so make sure you let your teammates know and support them as they go to take out the Stinger if it's needed.
