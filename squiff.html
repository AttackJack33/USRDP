The Squiffer

The Splattershot is a main weapon that is versatile in many different situations, even in Salmon Run. This main weapon is a middleweight weapon, meaning that it allows you to move just faster than heavyweight but not as fast as lightweight weapons. The Splattershot can be used in any situation with some exceptions. Examples are that it can be used to clear out lessers and bosses with ease, including egg delivery though it may not be recommended based on certain team combinations. Its modest damage and fire rate makes it a perfect all-rounder to take out almost every salmonid boss with ease with an exception of the Stinger as other weapons can splat Stingers more efficiently than the Splattershot but can still get the job done. Beware of Fly Fish though as going around splatting everything can leave you with a low ink tank, making you vulnerable to missiles, getting swarmed, and the inability to splat the said Fly Fish.

Role(s)

- Effective against bosses

- Handles crowd control
