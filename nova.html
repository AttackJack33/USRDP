The Splattershot Nova

The Splattershot Nova is a middleweight weapon that is honestly not too good compared to other weapons being that other weapons can do this weapons job but better. The Nova's damage and range can be compared to the Jet Squelcher but weaker in some aspects. With it's poor ability to splat many things with the exception of lessers, it is extremely ink efficient, allowing it to be a good painter, which is important to Salmon Run when it comes to moving around the map. It's moderate range can let it fight from a distance and take on some bosses. It is recommended to paint the ground for your team though along with softening up enemies and delivering eggs. Doing these things can help your team dramatically in the long run. Be careful around swarms though as being unaware of your surroundings can leave you to being splatted very quickly without a way out.

Role(s)

- Deliver Golden Eggs

- Paint the environment
