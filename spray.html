The Aerospray

The Areospray MG is a main weapon that has on of the best painting capabilities in Salmon Run. This weapon is a lightweight weapon meaning that yyou move the fastest in Salmon Run. The one part that the Aerospray shines in is painting the environment. Its ability to paint large amounts of turf in such short of a time is what makes it a very good utility for your team to be able to keep doing their work. Keeping the environment painted ensures that you and your teammates have accessible routes to deliver eggs or reach the shoreline. Its trade-off though is its poor range and very poor damage, but its extremely high fire rate makes it great at handling crowd control and delivering eggs with its high mobility but is also bad at taking out some technical bosses. It does have the ability to take down Scrappers, Slammin' Lids, Flipper Floppers, and Maws pretty well. Its high mobility also allows you to run in, get eggs, and paint the environment to give you and your teammates a route back to the basket. Do be aware of your ink tank though because while keeping the environment clean is important, it is also important to make sure you have enought ink to do some fighting as well.

Role(s)

- Deliver Golden Eggs

- Paint the environnment
