The Mini Splatling

The Mini Splatling is the "little brother" of the Heavy Splatling with the fastest charge rate and mobility of all the splatlings. This weapon surprisingly a middleweight weapon, meaning that you move faster than heavyweight but slower than lightweight. While the Mini Splatling has a lot of mobilility, it does not handle bosses very well with its low damage output and the amount it fires per charge. with it having a poor damage output, it can go toe to toe with lessers and sometimes large swarms due to its fast charge rate. The Mini Splatling's speed makes it a powerhouse upclose, making it great for CQC (Close Quarters Combat). This, along with its ability to charge fast helps deal with some bosses like Scrappers, Steel Eels, Maws, and Flipper Floppers. Its paint output is also acceptional, allowing it to take down Flipper Floppers faster than you'd think. Watch out for Steel Heads though as the bomb on its head cannot be reached from a safe distance unless you are on elevated ground.
