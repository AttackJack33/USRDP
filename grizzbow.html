The Grizzco Stringer

The Grizzco Stringer is an illegally modified version of the Tri-Stringer that boasts high damage and turfing capabilities with poor ink efficiency. This main weapon is a heavyweight, meaning that you have the slowest move speed in Salmon Run. The Grizzco Stringer has 9 shots compared to the usual 3 that are spread across a horizontal line with varying heights and proximity of each shot. Assuming all the shots connect, a tap shot will deal 315 damage and a full charge will deal a whopping 1350 damage, being one of the only few weapons that can tackle King Salmonids without the use of an Egg Launcher. Be careful though, though it can ink large amounts of turf and deal high damage, it can leave you vulnerable with a moderate charge rate and high ink consumption. Using all 9 shots fully charged at point-blank can 1-shot almost every Boss Salmonid in the game. Using the two shots on the right side that are very close to each other can give you the ability to hit Steelhead bombs and the pilot of a Slammin' Lid. Firing at the ground can be a good area denial tool because of its high turfing capabilities and the damage from the shot explosion.
